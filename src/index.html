The National Federation of Filipino American Associations (NaFFAA) Region 3-East (R3E) is pleased to invite you to its 2017 R3E Empowerment Conference (EC)! Attendees of this event will be community leaders and guests coming mainly from the five R3E member states, which include Michigan, Iowa, Indiana, Ohio, and Kentucky. NaFFAA Ohio will host this three-day event, which will focus on the theme, “Redefining the Future: Leadership – Mentoring – Partnership,” at the Hilton Columbus/Polaris in Columbus, Ohio, on July 28-30, 2017.
